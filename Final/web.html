TIGER SHROFF

Tiger Shroff is an Indian Movie Actor born in Mumbai, and has graduated from the American School of Bombay. He was a star athlete in school and won MVP awards routinely. Although he is a natural athlete and excels at any sport he gets into, his first passion is Martial Arts, having begun his training at the age of 5. He has studied Karate, Wushu and Taekwondo, and holds the Sho dan in Taekwondo. Apart from Martial Arts he is an avid practitioner of Parkour and Gymnastics. Tiger is currently the youngest Action Star in India, and has the biggest fan following amongst children in this country, who all look up to him as an example to follow. Tigers recent stints with MMA as an Owner of MMA Fight Team “Bengaluru Tigers” have led to him to be an avid fan and a practitioner of Mixed Martial Arts. He is the son of Ayesha and Jackie Shroff, and older brother to his sister, Krishna Shroff.

KRISHNA SHROFF

Krishna Shroff was born in Mumbai and completed her education at the American School of Bombay, followed by her graduation from SAE University in Dubai. She was always a star athlete in school, and won multiple awards including Female Athlete of the Year in her final year at School. Post-graduation, she produced a feature documentary on the transgender community in Mumbai, which went on to win multiple awards on the film festival circuit. She also worked as Assistant Director on a few movie projects. Krishna's passions are Football, Basketball and 24/7 Fitness Freak, with a new found aptitude for Mixed Martial Arts. Krishna has also worked at KOOH Sports and helped in developing their Basketball program for kids. She has coached the Youth basketball teams in various schools across India right from the grass root level of primary to high school. Krishna an MMA practitioner herself packs a mean punch as well. She is the daughter of Ayesha and Jackie Shroff and the younger sister of Tiger Shroff.
